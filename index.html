values

Transparency

Sharing is caring.. working !

I think we can agree that if we can talk about our difficulties in time, they're rarely becoming a problem.

Curiosity

I like to discover new skills, new methods and new tools, anecdotal or essential. I don't necessarily become an expert, but I like to open up the field of possibilities.

Commitment

Some subjects are close to my heart: animal protection since 2011 and gender equality since 2017.

Sociability

I like to find a form of camaraderie in teams, the best way for me to unite individuals towards a common goal

Scalability

There is a time to do and a time to evolve, I like to think about improving my methods. Making the same mistake over and over again is a frustration I can do without.

Empathy

Without overdoing it, I want to understand a minimum of the people I work with and for. Sometimes, taking the time to put yourself in their shoes allows me to avoid tensions and to facilitate communication.
